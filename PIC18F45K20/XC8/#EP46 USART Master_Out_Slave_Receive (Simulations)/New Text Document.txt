Welcome to this revamped mini series called PICbytes,

Here you will find various microcontroller tutorials using the PIC18F45K20 Microcontroller. 

In todays video we will simulate serial USART "RS232" communication between two master and slave microcontrollers.
Previously we had used 8 data lines connecting two microcontrollers toghether in a parallel bus configuration.
Resulting in their being some data output on the bargraph display connected to the slave. But this was inefficient and wasteful. 
Now using only one wire we will see how this can be done using the serial communication instead. 

Hint : In reality only the tx of the master needed to be connected to the rx of the slave. But in order to maintain completeness i connected them both.
