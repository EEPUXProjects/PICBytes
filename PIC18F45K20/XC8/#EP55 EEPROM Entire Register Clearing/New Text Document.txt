Welcome to this revamped mini series called PICbytes,

Here you will find various microcontroller tutorials using the PIC18F45K20 Microcontroller. 

In this video we find out how to clear the entire EEPROM register.
We will be using 0X00 as the value to cleanse the register. 
When completed a LED will iluminate to let you know when it is done.
