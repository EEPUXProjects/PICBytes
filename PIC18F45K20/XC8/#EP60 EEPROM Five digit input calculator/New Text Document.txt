Welcome to this revamped mini series called PICbytes,

Here you will find various microcontroller tutorials using the PIC18F45K20 Microcontroller. 

In this video we find out how to store 5 unique single digits into the EEPROM via the USART.
The special case here is that the values are read back and are totaled to give the final result.
