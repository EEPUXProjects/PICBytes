Welcome to this revamped mini series called PICbytes,

Here you will find various microcontroller tutorials using the PIC18F45K20 Microcontroller. 

In this video we will find out how to use a temperature sensor to control the speed of a DC motor. e.g Cooling Fan.
This example could be used in places where something needs to be cooled to a certain tempreture. 
Depending on the temperature , the speed of the dc fan is increased if it is getting warmer or decreased if the temperature is falling.
